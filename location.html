Our Location

The Institute of Technology Blanchardstown, is located in Blanchardstown, Dublin 15, about 15km west of Dublin City Centre.
GPS Co-ordinates for ITB are:
Latitude: 53.4051
Longitute: - 6.3779

Getting here by bus is simple too!
Dublin Bus serves the Blanchardstown area, including the transport hub at the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre. From here, the ITB Shuttle Bus will take you directly to ITB.
