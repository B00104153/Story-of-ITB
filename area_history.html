The local area surrounding The Institute of Technology has a rich historic background. The towns surrounding the Institute include Buzzardstown, Mulhuddart and Tyrrelstown etc.
All these towns are historic Irish Town Lands. Many years before the time The Institute of Technolgy was build, the land ITB stands on alongside all the different towns in the surrounding
area was hosts to a high number of large Irish families.
