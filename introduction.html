Introduction

Taking the Next Steps in Eduction

Welcome to the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown, soon to be known as TU Dublin.
The alliance of Institute of Technology Blanchardstown, Dublin Institute of Technology
and Institute of Technology Tallaght is set to become the first Technological University (TU Dublin) in Ireland.
TU Dublin will comprise a joined-up approach to the third-level experience that involves connecting students,
employers, researchers and the wider academic community to facilitate economic transformation and sustainable growth.
