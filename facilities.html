Sports Pavillion

The sport pavillion is a new addiction to the campus that adds a number of different facilities to the Institute.
The sports pavillion is not only a one stop for all sports related activities as it bulids onto the rest of the campus, providing students with more space to learn and pracistce their field of studies.
These facilities include

  • More lecture halls
  • A gym

    • The Linc Cafe

    The Linc Cafe in the Linc building is the perfect spot for a break for both students and staff. Located in the building next to the sport pavillion, the linc cafe provided a warm atmosphere paired with good food. The perfect quick stop in between classes or when taking a break.

    The Library

    The ITB library is a students one spot stop for everything! The student desk helps students with any quieries and problems that they may come across and is loceted right by the enterance. The library has two stories that provide students and staff with access to computers, printers and books.
